  • Rhethiel

    Rhethiel is a 2D RPG game that I developed for my capstone university project. I worked on this game in collaboration with two colleagues for about eight months. One of my colleagues and I focused primarily on the programming of the game, while the other colleague focused on the map design and layout.

    The games main systems are: A questing system, an inventory system, a randomly generating maze, a loot table system with a vast variety of unique items, and a number of elaborate econemy systems.
    Additionally it contains: 5 different environments each with unqiue monsters and an intricate boss, 40+ interactable NPCs, and an RNG treasure spawning system.
